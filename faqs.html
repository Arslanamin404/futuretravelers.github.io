Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

Can I book hotels through a travel website?
No, you can not book flights, hotels, rental cars, and other travel services directly through their platform. These services will be provided to you by Future-Travelers. One of our coordinator will assist you during tour.
Can I cancel or modify my booking through a travel website?
Yes, most travel websites allow you to cancel or modify your booking, but there may be fees associated with doing so.
What should I do if I encounter a problem with my booking?
If you encounter a problem with your booking, click on the contact option on the bottom of the website immediately. They should be able to assist you in resolving the issue.
What types of payment methods are accepted by travel websites?
We accept payments through credit cards, PayPal, UPI and sometimes bank transfers or other forms of payment. Check the website's payment options before booking.
Can I use a travel website to plan a multi-city trip?
Yes, you can plan and book multi-city trips, such as trips with multiple stops.
Are there any additional fees or charges when booking through a travel website?
Yes, some of our services charge additional fees or service charges when booking travel services. It is important to read the website's terms and conditions and to understand all fees and charges associated with your booking.
